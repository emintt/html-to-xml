Lesson 5: Collaboration Tools

There are multiple ways to collaborate with our teammates or co-workers; you need to decide for yourselves what works best. Some of them will be tools that you already use, some will not. Some require registration but this list, as this is created, is not exhaustive, it is merely a compilation of tools that are the most common at this time and are free or have a limited free version.

Here are just some of the ways that you can plan the different aspects of your collaboration:

Tools for online collaboration

  • Miro
  • Mural
  • Google products (Jamboard, Docs, Slides)
  • Padlet

Ways to stay in touch (text or video) with teammates

  • Slack
  • WhatsApp
  • Microsoft Teams
  • Google Meet
  • Zoom

Ways to organise your team’s work

  • Trello
  • Asana
  • Google Docs/Sheets

Tools for presentations for pitching

  • Google Slides
  • Canva
  • PowerPoint
